## Competitor Pricing Comparison (2026-04-04)

### Vagaro (Canada Pricing — CAD)
- **Model:** Per-calendar/per-staff pricing
- **Just Me (1 calendar):** $35/month
- **2 calendars:** $50/month
- **3 calendars:** $65/month
- **4+ calendars:** Incremental ~$15/calendar
- **7+ calendars:** Contact Sales
- **Multiple Locations:** Contact Sales
- **Free trial:** Yes
- **Cancellation:** Anytime, no fees
- **Add-ons:** Website builder, forms, marketing tools (extra cost)
- **Transaction fees:** Not disclosed on pricing page

### Fresha
- **Model:** Per-staff subscription + transaction fees
- **Individual plan:** $19.95/month (solo practitioner)
- **Team plan:** $14.95/per team member/month
  - 5 staff = ~$75/month
  - 10 staff = ~$150/month
- **Transaction processing:** 2.19% + $0.20 per card payment
- **Marketplace commission:** 20% fee on new clients from Fresha marketplace (min $6/new client)
- **Note:** Changed from free model to paid in 2025
- **Key difference:** Free for clients who book via your own website/Google/social — only charges for Fresha marketplace-sourced clients

### Waitwhile
- **Model:** Tiered plans by visit volume
- **Free:** $0/month — 1 location, up to 50 visits/month
- **Starter:** $31/month — up to 10 locations, 2,500 visits/month
- **Business:** $55/month — up to 10 locations, 5,000 visits/month
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing — unlimited
- **Annual discount:** 20% savings
- **SMS credits:** Free/Starter include $2 SMS credit to trial
- **No contracts:** Cancel anytime

### Pricing Gap Analysis

```
Your opportunity:

1. Vagaro charges per-calendar = expensive for multi-staff salons
   → You can charge flat rate per location

2. Fresha takes 20% commission on new clients from marketplace
   → You can charge 0% commission, pure SaaS model

3. Waitwhile caps visits (50 free, 2500 starter)
   → You can offer unlimited visits

4. NONE of them include AI receptionist or review management
   → These are add-on value you can bundle for free

Suggested pricing (undercut + more features):
- Solo: $29/month (vs Vagaro $35, Fresha $20)
- Team (up to 5): $99/month (vs Vagaro $110, Fresha $75)
- Unlimited: $199/month (vs Vagaro $200+, Fresha $150+)
- All plans include: AI Receptionist + Review Management + Waitlist
```

## G2 / Capterra Negative Review Analysis (2026-04-04)

### Vagaro — G2: 4.6/5 (383 reviews) | Capterra: 4.7/5 (3,400+ reviews)

**Top 5 Complaints:**
1. **軟體當機** — 付款中途登出、頁面載入慢造成排隊、有人被刪 400+ 客戶資料
2. **隱藏費用** — 基本功能額外收費、webhook $10/月、讀卡機 $100 提前解約費
3. **客服差** — 50+ 封信 + 20+ 通電話仍解決不了薪資問題、問題被歸類為「功能請求」
4. **付款處理失敗** — 交易顯示完成但實際沒扣款
5. **不好用** — 美甲店反映系統根本不適合、新員工難學

**客戶跳槽去哪：** Mangomint, Noterro, Zenoti

### Fresha — G2: 4.8/5 | Capterra: 4.8/5 (1,443 reviews)

**Top 5 Complaints:**
1. **20% 新客佣金被罵最慘** — 連 Google 搜到的客人也被收佣金、退款被拒、有人每月被收數百英鎊
2. **隱藏費用** — 每次加信用卡收 24p、SMS 另收費、總成本「失控不可預測」可達 70+ GBP/月
3. **客服態度差** — 被形容為「冷漠、沒有同理心」、感覺在跟機器人對話
4. **通知轟炸** — 半小時內收 4 次提醒 + 隔天再 2 次、客戶抱怨被打擾
5. **特定行業功能不足** — 按摩師覺得不適用、會員制轉換極度困難

**客戶跳槽原因：** 不可預測的佣金、5 年老客戶因客服差離開、建議找「固定月費」的替代品

### Waitwhile — G2: 4.6/5 | Capterra: 4.7/5 (63 reviews，很少)

**Top 5 Complaints:**
1. **排程只能 :00 和 :30** — 不支持 :15 間隔、對很多服務業不實用
2. **不能同步日曆** — 無法與外部日曆同步或匯出預約
3. **尖峰時段出 bug** — 最忙的時候系統最不穩定
4. **學習曲線陡** — 自訂設置複雜耗時、幾乎沒有教學文件
5. **定價變動逼升級** — Starter 方案限制大幅收緊、迫使用戶升到更貴方案

**客戶跳槽去哪：** Qminder, NextMe, WaitWell
