- Google uses four review signals for local rankings: volume (100+ reviews for competitive edge), rating (4.0+ minimum for local pack), recency (5-10 new reviews/week ideal), and response rate (80%+ target). Review keywords are increasingly important for AI Overview visibility.
- A fully optimized Google Business Profile is the foundation for review success. Key elements include proper NAP consistency, accurate categories, a direct review link, and strict compliance with Google's review policies — no incentives, no gating, no fakes.
